About Rentor

Launched in 2021, Rentor. , Amravatis's Leading Property Portal. Specially made for the Students by the Students. It mainly aims to help Students, Workers to find a suitable place in Amravati.It is an online fourm where House-Owners, and consumers can exchange information about real estate properties quickly, effectively and inexpensively.
